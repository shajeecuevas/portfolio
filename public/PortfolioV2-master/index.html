About

Raised by my mother in NY, my hometown of Manhattan would provide much of the sights and experiences that would help shape my world. During my travels, I was forced to mature quicker than my peers. I was constantly faced with new surroundings, new schools, and new sets of friends, and I found it difficult to become attached to anyone outside the family roots.

This nomadic life style gave me an opportunity, though, to broaden my mind and offered me many experiences from which to draw inspiration. I will one day share these experiences. I am determined to create my own identity that would, one day, be respected and loved by everyone.
